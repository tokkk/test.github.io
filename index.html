About

I am a born and bred North Londoner who moved to New York for a change of pace. I am immensely proud of my London roots and I make sure not to lose those ties, especially when it comes to music. Having grown-up listening to hip-hop and RnB from both sides of the Atlantic, I have a particular interest in helping to build the bridge between British and American sounds.

I've had the pleasure of meeting some truly inspiring artists, and am always on the look out for new stories to tell, whether they are of vocalists, DJs or producers. Interviewing is my ideal form of writing; I find the process of researching an artist, getting to know them and then sharing that story is engaging for both myself and the reader. I attend gigs regularly and will always take pleasure in covering live events, especially festivals where there's a larger atmosphere to convey and a range of genres to experience.

The goal is to share a discovery and hopefully help the artist in the process.
